What We Found

3 Things Holding Your School Back Online

01

Dated Blog-Style Homepage

Your homepage is a list of "Read more" cards with no hero image, no visual impact. Parents land on a wall of text instead of feeling the energy of a music school. First impressions drive enrollment.

02

No Online Enrollment

Parents must email to register — there's no class schedule viewer, no trial booking form. Modern parents expect to browse programs and sign up instantly, especially outside business hours.

03

Fragmented Information

Fees, term dates, programs, and contact details are scattered across multiple pages. Parents have to click through 4-5 pages to find basic information. Everything should be accessible in one clean flow.
